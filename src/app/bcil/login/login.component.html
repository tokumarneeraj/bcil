TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER OFFICE AT BCIL (TTO@BCIL)

TTO@BCIL has been set up with support from Department of Biotechnology - Biotechnology Industry Research Assistant Council - National Biopharma Mission (DBT-BIRAC-NBM). TTO@BCIL leverages three decades of experience of BCIL in technology transfer to undertake innovation management and technology transfer for all autonomous research institutes of DBT as well as all DBT funded extramural projects.

TTO@BCIL invites innovators and technology developers exploring Intellectual Property management and technology transfer prospects of their biotechnologies from all parts of the world.

Services Offered:

  • Transfer of promising Technology(ies) to suitable companies in India and abroad for translation and commercialization
  • Robust IP Management including IP assessment, IP filing and prosecution in time bound manner.
  • Forging national and international collaborations, Public Private Partnerships including industry sponsored research.
  • Entrepreneurship development

With Support from DBT-BIRAC-NBM
